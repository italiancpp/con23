Take the Metro

Metro line B

Basilica S. Paolo Station

This is the preferred way to reach the location, only a few minutes walk from the university.

The line serves the city center, should you need to find accomodation in that area.

Please refer to the Metro Service website as well as the Rome Transportation Company for maps, timetables and up to date information on strikes and other service interruptions

By Train

Coming by train may help you reach Rome in the morning and return home in the evening, should you wish to do so, with no need for accomodation.

The Metro B line connects both Termini and Tiburtina stations to the location.

Please refer directly to the websites of the two major italian railway companies:

  • Trenitalia
  • Italo

By Car

Road traffic in Rome is notoriously treacherous, even more so during the morning rush hour. Parking spaces are highly sought after real estate.

It should be possible to park in streets around the univeristy:

  • Via Vito Volterra
  • Via Angelo Battelli
  • Via Salvatore Pincherle

The University's parking lot will NOT be available for the attendees.

By bus

The University area is served by numerous bus lines.

Please refer to the Rome Transportation Company website to find the closest to your departure point.
